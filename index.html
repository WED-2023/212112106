About Me

Osman Musa Yosef Showgan was born in the year 1936 in the Circassian village of Kfar Kama, British Mandate Palestine(Now Israel).
Kfar Kama was established by Circassians who's origins are from the Black Sea Coast of Circassia (Today labled as Krasnodar Krai under Russia), in the 1870's following the Circassian Genocide and expulsion from the native homeland.
Read more about the Circassian Genocide Here

Osman married Shazia Musa Hadish (born in the year 1940) and they had 5 children. Osman worked hard to provide for his family, working various jobs such as a Truck Driver, a carpenter and more for long hours.
Osman passed away from cancer when I was about 2 years old (in the year 2003), He was known as a kind and loving person.

Q&A

1) Explain 3 Key Differences between Web1, Web2, and Web3

Web1 (Static Web): Read-only, static web pages, no user interaction.

Web2 (Interactive Web): Read-write, user-generated content, social media, centralized platforms.

Web3 (Decentralized Web): Read-write-own, decentralized applications (DApps), blockchain-based.

2) Explain the differences from Question 1 using an Example Use-Case

Note: We will take the Use-Case of searching the query "Side effects of Medications"


Web1: A static HTML page listing side effects without user interaction.

Web2: An interactive platform like Wikipedia or Reddit where users can discuss and add comments.

Web3: A decentralized medical data-sharing system where AI and blockchain verify and personalize the answer.
